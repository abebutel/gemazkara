Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to the Azkara website and mobile application.

1. Data Collection

We believe in your privacy. The Azkara app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, user data, or tracking analytics. All information entered into the app (such as names and dates for generating booklets) is processed entirely locally on your device and is never sent to our servers.

2. Permissions

The app may request access to your device's native sharing or printing capabilities solely for the purpose of allowing you to export or share your generated booklets. We do not track or log these actions.

3. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party advertising or tracking frameworks.

4. Changes to This Policy

If our data practices change in the future, we will update this privacy policy and publish the revised version here.
